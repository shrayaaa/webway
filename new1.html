Training & Placement Cell

Our Training & Placement (T&P) Cell bridges students with industry through continuous skill-building, internships, and on-campus recruitment. We collaborate with top recruiters, run finishing-school programs, and track outcomes to ensure every graduate is job-ready.

Objectives & Roles

  • Organize aptitude, coding, and soft-skill training across semesters.
  • Build industry partnerships and coordinate internships & campus drives.
  • Mentor students on profiles, resumes, and interview preparation.
  • Maintain placement statistics, MoUs, and recruiter feedback.

General Policies

  • Students must register with the T&P Cell to participate in placements.
  • Professional discipline and punctuality are expected during all drives.
  • Once placed, students may not be allowed in further recruitment processes.
  • The T&P Cell reserves the right to update policies when required.

Infrastructure & Facilities

  • Dedicated interview cabins and GD rooms with recording support.
  • Computer labs with coding platforms and test facilities.
  • Smart seminar hall for pre-placement talks & workshops.
  • Recruiter lounge, Wi-Fi, parking, and hospitality support.
